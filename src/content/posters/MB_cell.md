This study investigates the developmental origins and metabolic programs of medulloblastoma by integrating bulk and single-cell transcriptomic datasets from pediatric brain tumors and the developing cerebellum. My contributions included differential expression analysis, pathway module scoring, and visualization of glycolysis and oxidative phosphorylation signatures across medulloblastoma, posterior fossa A (PFA), cerebellopontine angle (CPA) tumors, and normal cerebellar cell populations, helping characterize subtype-specific metabolic states and their relationship to normal cerebellar development.
